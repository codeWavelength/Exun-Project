SAFETY GUIDELINES




  1. Do's
    • Always stand in a que
    • limit your baggage to 15kgs.
    • Stand on the left side of escalators and while moving keep to the right
    • Move away from escalators immediately after use
  2. Dont's
    • Do not bring eatable inside the station premises
    • Do not bring pets inside
    • Do not smoke
    • Do not travel without your smart card or token
    • Do not jump over the ticket barrier
    • Do not share your card or token with another person on the same journey
    • Do not cross the yellow line on the platform
    • Do not step onto the Metro tracks
    • Do not travel on the roof of the train
    • Do not try to open the doors forcibly
    • Do not deface or put up posters inside the Metro station or train
